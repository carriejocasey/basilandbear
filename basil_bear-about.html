The plant, sweet basil, was known to symbolize good or best wishes. It is also said to “procure a cheerful and merry heart.”

Bears are symbolic of wisdom and inner power. The bear spirit encourages one to enjoy their journey; to truly delight in the sweetness of life’s little enjoyments.

Both of these elements are strong representations of my brand and work. I care deeply about others and always hope to inspire a cheerful and merry heart in those I come into contact with. I value compassion and joy. Sending good wishes to others is something I do often, whether it is in the form of a letter or a quilt. At Basil & Bear, I resonate deeply with taking the time to slow down and enjoy the journey. Delighting in the little things along the way, can often be more important than the final destination. I strive continuously to be growing in a way that will fill me with wisdom and inner power.

I want to enable people to share their stories, create magic moments, be humble leaders, and extend grace and love to all.
