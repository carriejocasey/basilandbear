lucy in the sky

Lucy in the Sky is a pop-up book based on the lyrics from Lucy in the Sky with Diamonds by The Beatles. The concept behind this book was to explore and learn the capabilities and boundaries of paper manipulation and pop-ups. The design of Lucy in the Sky utilizes typography to display the lyrics of the song. The artwork was created by manipulating and layering photographs in photoshop to create a psychedelic spread representing each set of lyrics.
