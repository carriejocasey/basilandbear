basil & bear
design co.

EST. 2015

Hi! Welcome to Basil & Bear Design Co., a small design shop based in Boston and run by, Carrie Casey (that's me)!

I have a background in print and production design and a passion for all things texture, pattern, and color. I'll never abandon my tactile roots but I'm beginning to truly embrace the digital world we are living in. I currently work at a startup in Boston where I've fallen in love with product and user interface design! Designing for web applications, mobile applications, and websites has been an exciting turn of events! My hobbies include all of the crafts. You can usually find me on the weekends sewing quilts and weird pieces of clothing or making homemade cards and sending them snail-mail-style to the wonderful people in my life!

Although I'm currently employed, I love collaborating with people! Design is story telling and I'm here to help you tell yours. If you're interested in working together, I'd love to hear from you. Let's connect!
