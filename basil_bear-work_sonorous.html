sonorous

Tactile Recordings is a fictional recording studio located in Seattle, WA. Tactile’s aim is to reconnect with music’s roots, to discover the tactile quality and the warmth that is entwined with music. Tactile produces music and records only to vinyl to pay tribute to the physicality of music. The branding of Tactile extends beyond simple logo and identity design and moves into digital form as a responsive website. Tactile produces a quarterly publication, Sonorous, a magazine that explores and makes discoveries about the seattle music scene. Sonorous is a collaboration between artists, musicians, writers, and photographers.
