[mostly]about a boy(s)

[mostly] aboutaboy(s) is a self-authored, limited edition of six books containing twenty poems that I wrote between 2009 and 2013. Each book was sewn with a coptic stitch and bound as a modified Gary Frost. The end sheets have been letterpressed with a pattern of poppies, which is also blind embossed on all of the inside pages, as well as printed on the cover.

(from) the passion #4

they say that every snowflake is different. / if that were true, how could we recover / from the wonder of it? / what you risk reveals what you value. / does it matter whom you lose to, / if you lose?

existence

your shape was / the same / as it had always been / only surreal / because i convinced / myself that you / never existed / but you do / and i took in the / sight of you / from afar / before approaching / cautiously / you turned / your eyes lit / up and a smile / grew on your / face / .regardless. / of the fact / that you no longer / exist

fall.

how quickly / the light fades / the excitement / wears off / how quickly / the present / becomes / the past / how quickly / we fall / right back / into place / how quickly / it all comes / tumbling / down.
